Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 2026

Lazarus Labs (“we”, “our”, “us”) is committed to respecting and protecting your privacy. This privacy policy explains how our mobile applications handle information.

1. Information We Collect

Our applications do not collect, store, or process any personally identifiable information. There is no account creation, login, or user profile within our apps.

However, our applications may use third-party services that automatically collect certain types of data for advertising, analytics, crash reporting, or performance measurement. This data is collected by the third party—not by Lazarus Labs.

2. Third-Party Services

Our apps may integrate with the following third-party services:

  • Google AdMob – used for displaying ads. AdMob may collect:
    • Device identifiers (e.g., IDFA/AAID)
    • Approximate location (based on IP address)
    • App interaction data (e.g., ad views, clicks)
    • Crash and performance data
    This data is processed in accordance with Google’s Privacy Policy:
    https://policies.google.com/privacy

  • Google Play Services – required for Android functionality. May collect diagnostic data.
  • Apple App Store Services – may collect analytics and crash logs on iOS devices.

These third-party services may use collected data to personalize advertising, measure ad performance, or for fraud prevention. Lazarus Labs does not receive or store this data.

3. Cookies and Tracking

Our apps do not use cookies. Third-party services such as Google AdMob may use tracking technologies to deliver and measure advertisements.

4. Data Retention

Since we do not collect or store personal data, we retain no user information. Any retention of diagnostic or advertising data is managed solely by the third-party services listed above.

5. Security

We take privacy seriously and design our apps so that no personal data is handled directly by us. Security for any data collected by third-party services is governed by their respective privacy policies.

6. Your Consent

By using our apps, you consent to the processing of data by the third-party services integrated into the application, as described in this policy.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Updates will be posted on this page with a revised “Last Updated” date. We encourage you to review this page periodically.
