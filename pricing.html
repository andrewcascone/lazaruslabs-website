Good to know

Pricing questions, answered.

Why a range instead of one number?

Every project has different complexity. The estimator gives an honest ballpark; after a quick call I'll send a fixed quote so you know exactly what you're paying.

What makes a project cost more?

The big drivers are user accounts, payment processing, and inventory/marketplace logic — anything with a backend and data. Building for both iOS and Android also adds to the total.

How do payments work?

Typically 50% to begin and 50% on launch, with larger projects split into milestones. No surprise invoices.

Do you offer ongoing support?

Yes — optional monthly maintenance plans cover updates, hosting, store re-submissions, and small changes after launch.
